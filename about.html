Joed - A Seattle Based EDM Artist/Producer


This project stems from my innate passion for Electronic Dance Music (EDM). Inspired by groundbreaking and transcendent artists such as Avicii, Kygo, and Petit Biscuit, Joed is a project that blends in traditional progressive/tropical house with emotional-melodic synths and future-bass elements. I create and discover a world a of sounds through Image Line’s digital audio workstation (DAW) known as FL Studio. I plan to create a difference and bring an innovative and unique sounds of electronic music to Seattle.

I think music is best described as a world of feelings that we can create through audio design. Being able to create a feeling that never existed before is an amazing concept in itself as a result of what one creates. My goal is to discover as many feelings and moods out there that makes the listener feel all types of happy, nostalgia, and euphoria.
